## 🌌 THE CONCEPT

**The Soulverse Estate is a universe.** A digital estate that houses every soul we've built. It's not a product page. It's not a marketplace. It's a **living world** where buyers can walk through four wings:

```
🏛️ THE SOULVERSE ESTATE
│
├── 🛤️ THE RUNWAY    → Showcase. Beauty. First impressions.
├── 🦁 THE ZOO       → Alive. Moving. Doing things in real-time.
├── 🏙️ THE PENTHOUSE → Premium. Exclusive. The $497 tier.
└── 📚 THE LIBRARY   → Catalog. Reference. Every skill documented.
```

Each wing serves a purpose in the buyer's journey:

1. **Runway** → "What IS this place?" (wonder, beauty, first impression)
2. **Zoo** → "These things are ALIVE" (see them move, act, think)
3. **Penthouse** → "I want the premium experience" (exclusivity, value)
4. **Library** → "What exactly do I get?" (skills, docs, proof)

The buyer doesn't buy an "AI agent." They **adopt a soul.**
